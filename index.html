Glossary

Nutri-Score

A–E rating of overall nutritional quality based on calories, sugar, saturated fat, sodium, fiber, and protein per 100g. A is best, E is worst.

NOVA Group

Food processing classification: 1 = unprocessed, 2 = culinary ingredients, 3 = processed foods, 4 = ultra-processed. Lower is better.

Eco-Score

A–E rating of environmental impact considering carbon footprint, packaging, transport, and land use. A is lowest impact, E is highest.

Nutrient Levels

Low / Moderate / High ratings for fat, saturated fat, sugars, and salt per 100g, based on EU reference thresholds.

NOVA 4 — Ultra-Processed

Foods with five or more ingredients, additives, and industrial substances not normally used in home cooking (e.g. emulsifiers, flavors, colorants).
